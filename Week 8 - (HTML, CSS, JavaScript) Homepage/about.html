About

a Pianist, a Composer, ......a CS Student...

Born in Malaysia, Leon Kwan graduated with the highest honours from TU Dublin Conservatoire of Music in Ireland. He studied piano performance with international pianist and Irish Music specialist, Dr. Una Hunt, and studied music composition with Dr. David Bremner.

Achievements in his Bachelor's:

  1. Performed in the National Concert Hall in Dublin for “The Celebration of Irish Pianist Composers”, presented by the DIT Department of Keyboard Studies in Feb 2017.
  2. Won 1st Prize in Marie Jones Award Competition, presented by DIT Conservatory of Music in March 2017.
  3. Won 1st Prize in Senior Duet Competition, presented by DIT Conservatory of Music in March 2017.
  4. Won 2nd Prize in Tomás Ó Súilleabháin Award Competition, presented by DIT Conservatory of Music in March 2017.
  5. Won 1st Prize in Sidney Grieg Memorial Trophy Competition (Organ), presented by DIT Conservatory of Music in March 2018.
  6. Set up and led a Student-led Contemporary Music Ensemble and supposed to perform two concerts in April 2020. (Cancelled due to Covid-19 outbreak)
  7. Awarded First-Class Honours in both composing and performing exams in final year in October 2020.
